Mark Simkin

I am currently a postdoc in the Cryptography and Security Group at Aarhus University, where I am advised by Claudio Orlandi. In October 2019 I received my PhD at Aarhus University, where I was advised by Ivan Damgård and co-advised by Claudio Orlandi.
